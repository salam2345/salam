Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1985

Abah Farm was established over three decades ago with a simple mission: to produce the finest dairy products while respecting our animals and the environment. What started as a small family operation has grown into a beloved local institution, but we've never lost sight of our core values.

Our herd of happy, healthy cows roam freely on lush pastures and are fed a natural diet without artificial additives. This care and attention to detail is reflected in the exceptional quality and taste of our products.

Today, the second generation of our family continues this tradition, blending time-honored methods with modern sustainable practices to bring you dairy as nature intended.
